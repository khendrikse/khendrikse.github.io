About me

My name is Karin, I am a full-time Communications Adviser learning how to code in her free time. I have always been obsessed by the internet and used to create webpages using HTML and a little bit of CSS during high school for me and my friends. After high-school I studied graphic design and majored in photography. Following my graduation I continued to study communications, using my graphic skills whenever possible.

Since the summer of 2017 I have actively picked up learning how to program again during my free time. After mastering the basics of HTML and CSS again, I am now working on small projects to better understand Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, Databases, getting my digital literacy up-to-date and just to have fun.
